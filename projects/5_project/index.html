Inexorable Shifting 2 (2019)

A piece for autonomous complex adaptive system


Inexorable Shifting 2 is the adaptive version of a work developed in 2013. The piece consists of an autonomous self-oscillating feedback delay network of frequency shifting units. Whereas the original work had fixed shift values for each of the node in the network, this version performs a time-domain spectral centroid analysis to determine the direction of the frequency shift and implement counterbalancing mechanisms.
